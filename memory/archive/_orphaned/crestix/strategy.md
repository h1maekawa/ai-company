# Crestix OS Strategy

## Objectives
- Develop AI Company OS MVP to streamline internal workflows and task orchestration.
- Build a robust foundation (AI OS v3.0) incorporating dual Personal + Corporate environments.
- Keep the system highly automated, simple, and clean to prevent over-engineering.

## Product Focus
- **Personal OS**: Maximize individual monetization (Note business + Investment portfolio).
- **Crestix OS**: Build autonomous agents, pipelines, and tools for eventual B2B enterprise offerings.
